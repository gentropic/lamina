GCU Lamina — Privacy Policy

GCU Lamina (“lamina”) is a local, offline data-file viewer, built to do nothing on the network.

We collect nothing

Lamina has no accounts, no sign-in, no telemetry, no analytics, and no cookies or trackers. We do not collect, receive, or have any access to information about you or your use of the app.

Nothing leaves your device

Lamina runs under a Content-Security-Policy of connect-src 'none', which makes every network request — uploads, downloads, beacons — impossible. The files you open are read and processed entirely on your own device; their contents are never uploaded, transmitted, or sent to us or anyone else.

Local-only storage

For your convenience, lamina may keep small items on your device — a list of recently opened files and saved view settings (.lamina lenses) — in your browser’s or operating system’s local storage. This stays on your device, is never transmitted, and you can clear it at any time (Recents can be disabled and cleared from within the app).

No personal data, by construction

Because lamina runs entirely offline and transmits nothing, it cannot gather personal information. You can verify this yourself: disconnect from the network and it still works; inspect network activity and you’ll see zero requests. See gentropic.org/security for the capability declaration and how to verify it.
